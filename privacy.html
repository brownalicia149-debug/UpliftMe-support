UpliftMe Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025

Owner: Alicia Brown

App Name: UpliftMe

1. Introduction

UpliftMe is a wellness and journaling app designed to support reflection and personal growth. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

2. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

  • Journal entries, notes, and other content you create in the app
  • Optional profile information such as your name or email (if you choose to provide it)

b. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (such as iPhone model and iOS version)
  • App usage information and performance data
  • Crash logs used to diagnose and fix technical issues

We do not intentionally collect your precise location, financial information, or contacts.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the UpliftMe app
  • To save and display your journal entries and content
  • To improve stability, performance, and user experience
  • To troubleshoot issues and respond to support requests

Your journal content is used only to provide the app’s core functionality and is not sold or shared for advertising.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

We may share limited information only in these situations:

  • With service providers that help us operate the app (for example, analytics or crash reporting tools), who are required to protect your data
  • To comply with legal obligations, respond to valid legal requests, or protect our rights and users

5. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption and secure storage where applicable. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your information for as long as your account or app data is active or as needed to provide the UpliftMe service.

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at upliftmeapp.support@gmail.com. We will delete your personal data and journal content within a reasonable time, typically within 7 days, unless we are required to keep it longer for legal reasons.

7. Children’s Privacy

UpliftMe is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will delete it.

8. International Users

Your information may be processed and stored in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. By using UpliftMe, you consent to the transfer of information to these locations, which may have different data protection laws than your country.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the “Effective Date” at the top of this page. Your continued use of the app after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
